# LazyQR - A Webcam QR Reader
This is LazyQR, a QR code reader made for your webcam or laptop cam when you are too lazy to do a qr code form on your phone. 

When the camera feed opens, hold up your QR code, ideally on your phone or a large sheet of paper, to the camera. When it is read, use the terminal pop-up to either open the link in your default web browser or close it, thereby increasing security. 

A more convenient way of accessing the application will be coming, along with a possible UI.
